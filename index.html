Lessons

I work hard to make driving lessons a balance of being enjoyable, interesting and fun, yet challenging enough to promote and encourage learning with the emphasis on developing safe, skilful and confident driving habits. I teach across the full spectrum of students from young people with no experience to advanced leaner’s ‘fine tuning’ in preparation for their test. I also teach selective refresher lessons for qualified drivers on manoeuvres such as ‘Parallel parking, Bay parking and reversing.’

All lessons are individually planned to suit the ability and progress of the student and start with:

  • Recap - Progress made the previous lesson,
  • Objectives - What we are aiming to achieve in the lesson,
  • Briefing - How we meet the objectives,
  • Feedback - How the lesson went, did we meet the objectives.

Single lessons are an hour in length but are normally paired to make a total of two hours although are flexible to the ability of the individual. Intensive courses can also be arranged, again length and time of lessons are adaptable and flexible to the needs and abilities of the student.

Pass Plus

I am registered to offer the Pass Plus driving scheme, this is a mini course that offers six modules with a minimum of six hours tuition, focussing and further developing driving skills in the following areas:

  • In town
  • In all weathers
  • On rural roads
  • At night
  • On dual carriageways
  • On motorways

The course is aimed primarily at newly qualified drivers but can be accessed by anyone with a full licence. There are no formal exams to pass but ongoing assessments are carried out by the instructor and all modules must be successfully completed to the required standard. Insurance companies can offer a discounted premium to drivers who have successfully completed the course but check with your own company. The Pass Plus course is offered at the normal hourly fee of £20. Further information from the DirectGov website.
